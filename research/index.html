Matthew Kvalheim     Research

Research

My research interests include nonlinear systems in general, and in particular: dynamical systems, control theory, stochastic processes, and robotics.

Specific topics related to my work: feedback stabilization, Koopman operator theory, data-driven algorithms, closed one-forms in dynamics, Morse/Conley theory, stochastic processes and topology, invariant manifolds, bifurcations, differential flatness, geometric mechanics, and hybrid systems.
