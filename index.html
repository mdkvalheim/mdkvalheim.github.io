I am a postdoctoral assistant professor in the Department of Mathematics at the University of Michigan. My research interests include nonlinear systems in general, and in particular: dynamical systems, control theory, stochastic processes, and robotics. I taught two sections of Calculus I (Math 115) in Fall 2022, and I am teaching two sections of Differential Equations (Math 316) in Winter 2023.
